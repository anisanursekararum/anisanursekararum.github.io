QA-Hub - Intelligent QA Control Panel

Elevating my "vibe coding" journey, I tackled a critical real-world problem: Quality Assurance is often undervalued as just a manual checkpoint before release. To change this, I re-engineered the entire QA workflow into QA-Hub, an intelligent and centralized QA control panel.

By leveraging AI-powered RAG, QA-Hub slashes test case creation from days to under 10 minutes, using smart backend prompting to match existing cases before generating new ones.

  • Phase 1-2 (Done): Test Case & Run Management, AI RAG Parse Engine, SMTP integration.
  • Phase 3-4 (In Progress): Headless Playwright test automation with self healing scripts.
  • Phase 5-8 (Planning): Load/performance testing, pentesting, and advanced LLM embeddings.
